Blue Velvet

Actors: Kyle MacLachlan, Laura Dern, Dennis Hopper

Characters:

  • Jeffrey Beaumont
  • Sandy Williams
  • Frank Booth

Blue Velvet is a mystery thriller directed by David Lynch. It follows Jeffrey Beaumont, who discovers a severed ear while in his hometown, which leads him to become involved with singer Dorothy Vallens as well as the criminal Frank Booth, who abducted her husband and son in order to force her into slavery.
